Manchester United is a team based on the city of Manchester, United Kingdom. We are called the Red Devils or the Reds. We are build by our history of champions and amazing players or legends like Cristiano Ronaldo, David Beckham, Wayne Rooney, Eric Cantona, and many more. Our stadium is called the Theather of Dreams or Old Trafford. They compete in the Premier League, the top tier of English football. They were founded as Newton Heath LYR Football Club in 1878, but changed their name to Manchester United in 1902. After a spell playing in Clayton, Manchester, the club moved to their current stadium, Old Trafford, in 1910. Domestically, Manchester United have won a joint-record twenty top-flight league titles, thirteen FA Cups, six League Cups and a record twenty-one FA Community Shields. Additionally, in international football, they have won the European Cup/UEFA Champions League three times, and the UEFA Europa League, the UEFA Cup Winners' Cup, the UEFA Super Cup, the Intercontinental Cup and the FIFA Club World Cup once each.
